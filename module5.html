Days 22–23
When Resistance Returns — What To Do

"Change is not linear. Growth does not move in a straight line upward. The difficult day is not the old belief winning. It is the old belief leaving."

Three-Step Protocol When Resistance Returns
Step 1
Recognise it
Name the old thought: "This is the old belief. This is not who I am now." Recognition removes its power instantly.
Step 2
Redirect or replace
Move immediately to any I Love affirmation. If resistance remains, switch to something you genuinely love. Do not feed the old thought with attention.
Step 3
Break the pattern
If nothing shifts — change what you are doing. Stand up. Walk. Put music on. The mind follows the body.
