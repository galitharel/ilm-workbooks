The Sleep Practice

The Most Powerful Window
Week 3 · Day 21 of 7

Before You Begin · Day 21

In theta, the logical brain begins to shut down. The critical faculty goes quiet. Whatever you impress on the subconscious in this state is received without resistance.

Tonight — your last thought is your most powerful one.

The Sleep Sequence
1. Release
Lie comfortably. Three slow breaths. Let the body soften completely.
2. Begin
Repeat your chosen I Love affirmation softly in your mind. Like a gentle lullaby.
3. Follow
As expansion builds, allow images to arrive naturally. Do not force anything.
4. Drift
Let the affirmation and feeling be your last conscious thought. Fall asleep inside it.
