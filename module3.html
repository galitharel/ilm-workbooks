Lesson 3.4
The I Love Redirect

The Redirect Practice

The goal of the I Love Redirect is not to fight your thoughts. It is not to push away fear, doubt, or old patterns.

It is to create a new response.

When an old thought appears, you are practicing something powerful: Pause. Notice. Choose Love.

Practice
The Pause Practice
Notice → Breathe → Choose Love
Step 1
Notice
"I am having this thought." Do not judge it. Do not argue with it. Simply notice.
Step 2
Create Space
Take one breath. Place your attention on your body. Ask: "What am I feeling right now?"
Step 3
Choose Your Redirect
Say "I love…" then move toward a thought that creates a feeling of openness, warmth, or appreciation.
