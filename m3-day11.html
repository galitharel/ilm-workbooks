Catching the Old Thoughts in Real Time
Week 2 · Day 11 of 7

Before You Begin · Day 11

Today you learn to catch old thoughts as they arise — and redirect them without fighting them.

The goal is not to eliminate old patterns. It is to create a new response.

Lesson 3.4
The I Love Redirect
Notice → Breathe → Choose Love
Step 1
Notice
"I am having this thought." Do not judge it. Simply notice.
Step 2
Create Space
Take one breath. Place your attention on your body. Ask: "What am I feeling right now?"
Step 3
Choose Your Redirect
Say "I love…" and move toward a thought that creates openness, warmth, or appreciation.
