"In theta, the logical brain begins to shut down. The critical faculty goes quiet. The RAS stops scanning for proof. Whatever you impress on the subconscious in this state is received without resistance."

The Nightly Transition Ritual

Sleep is not only the end of your day. It is the doorway into tomorrow.

The Sleep Sequence
1. Release
Lie comfortably. Three slow breaths. Let the body soften completely. You are arriving.
2. Begin
Repeat your chosen I Love affirmation softly in your mind. Like a gentle lullaby.
3. Follow
As expansion builds, allow images to arrive naturally. Do not force anything.
4. Drift
Let the affirmation and feeling be your last conscious thought. Fall asleep inside it.
