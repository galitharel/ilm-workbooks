Three-Step Protocol When Resistance Returns
Step 1
Recognise it
Name the old thought. Recognition removes its power instantly.
Step 2
Redirect or replace
Move immediately to any I Love affirmation. Do not feed the old thought with attention.
Step 3
Break the pattern
If nothing shifts — change what you are doing. Stand up. Walk. The mind follows the body.
