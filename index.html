A Message From Galit
Before You Begin

I want to start by saying something important: the fact that you are here means something.

Not just that you purchased a course. It means that some part of you still believes change is possible. Even if another part of you has tried before and doubted. Even if you have been here — standing at the edge of transformation — and found yourself back where you started.

I know that feeling. I lived it for years.

I did Vipassana retreats. I meditated every single day. I worked with coaches. I repeated affirmations until they felt hollow. And no matter what I did, something inside me kept saying: liar.

The problem was never me. And it is not you.

The problem was the method. And that is exactly what this course gives you — a different one.

The I Love Method™ does not ask you to force a new belief. It does not ask you to repeat words until your mind accepts them. It asks you to do something completely different: to create a bridge between where you are and where you want to be — built entirely from love.

Over the next 30 days, you will learn how to work with your mind instead of against it. You will understand your resistance instead of fighting it. You will discover that the same energy you have been using to push away the old belief — is the same energy that, redirected, creates the new one.

Go slowly. There is no rush. One day at a time is exactly right.

I am so glad you are here.

With love, Galit 🖤
How This Works
Your 30-Day Journey

One Day at a Time

Each day you will watch a short lesson, complete a journaling practice, and finish with a reflection. Some days take 20 minutes. Others may take longer — not because the content is complex, but because something real is happening inside you and you want to stay with it.

That is not resistance. That is the method working.

  • Complete each day before moving to the next
  • Complete each module before unlocking the next one
  • Your journal answers save automatically in your browser
  • You can always return to any day you have completed
